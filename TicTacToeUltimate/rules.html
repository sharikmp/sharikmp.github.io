Game Rules

  • Only one mini board can be active at a time
  • If player 1 select a move in upper right corner of mini board, then player 2 is bound to play in upper right corner mini board only
  • In this way, the players need to play move keeping in mind opponet's next move as well
  • Wooo!, this makes the tradition Tic Tac Toe game interesting
  • And also decreases the chances of game draw!
