How to Play

  • A secret cipher maps each animal symbol to a letter — shown in the CIPHER KEY.
  • Each round presents a coded word as animal symbols in letter slots.
  • Tap an animal in the cipher key to select it, then tap an empty slot to place the letter.
  • Use the 4 multiple-choice options to guess the entire word at once.
  • Guess early before the timer runs out for maximum points!
  • Wrong multiple-choice guess: −20 points and word advances.
  • Decode 5 words per sprint. Best score wins!
