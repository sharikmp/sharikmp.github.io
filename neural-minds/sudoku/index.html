How to Play

  • Fill every empty cell with a digit 1–9.
  • Each row, column, and 3×3 box must contain every digit exactly once.
  • Tap an empty cell to open the number picker.
  • Tap a number to place it — or ✕ Erase to clear.
  • A wrong placement counts as a mistake and adds a +30s time penalty to your score.
  • The puzzle has one unique solution — use logic, not guessing!
  • Lower score (time + penalties) is better. Aim for zero mistakes!
