Game Rules


  • Only one mini board can be active at a time

  • If player 1 plays a move in upper right corner of mini board, then player 2 is bound to play in upper right corner mini board only

  • In this way, the players need to play move keeping in mind opponet's next move as well

  • Wooo!, this makes the tradition Tic Tac Toe game interesting, and also decreases the chances of game draw.

  • In case, a mini board is draw, it's consider as won by both players.

  • Player won the game if there is a cross in bigger board with mini board,
