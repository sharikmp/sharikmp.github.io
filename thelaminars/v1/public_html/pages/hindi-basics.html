Hindi – Basics

Foundational Hindi language skills for beginners of all ages — reading, writing, and spoken basics in a warm, supportive environment.

Program Overview

Hindi Basics is an introductory program open to learners of all ages who want to start learning Hindi from scratch or strengthen their foundational skills. Whether you're a child just beginning to read, an adult wanting to communicate better, or a non-Hindi speaker curious about the language — this course is for you.

The program builds confidence step by step — starting from the Hindi alphabet (Varnamala), progressing through reading and writing, and then moving into basic conversational practice. Sessions are friendly, patient, and entirely beginner-focused.

What You'll Learn

Varnamala

Hindi alphabet, vowels & consonants

Reading & Writing

Words, sentences & short passages

Basic Grammar

Nouns, verbs, basic sentence structure

Conversation

Everyday phrases & spoken practice

What Makes It Special

True Beginner-Friendly

Zero prior knowledge assumed. We start from the very first letters and build up at a comfortable pace.

Open to All Ages

Children, teenagers, working adults — anyone who wants to learn Hindi is welcome.

Conversational Practice

Beyond reading and writing, students practice speaking everyday Hindi to build real-world comfort.

Ideal for Non-Hindi Speakers

Specially suited for learners from other language backgrounds who want to pick up Hindi naturally.

Who Is This For?

  • Young children being introduced to Hindi for the first time
  • Adults who never formally learned Hindi and want to start now
  • Non-Hindi speakers wanting to learn the language for work or personal reasons
  • Students who want to solidify weak Hindi basics before joining an academic course
