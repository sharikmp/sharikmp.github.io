Program Overview

The Laminars Little Minds program is designed specifically for students in Classes 1 through 5. At this crucial developmental stage, we focus on building strong academic foundations while keeping learning joyful, engaging, and effective.

Every child learns differently. Our small batch sizes and individual-attention approach ensure that no child is left behind — whether they're breezing ahead or need extra support.
