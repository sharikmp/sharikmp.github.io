Program Overview

The Laminars Foundation Academy is tailored for Classes 6 to 8 — a critical transition period in every student's academic journey. This program deepens subject knowledge, develops analytical thinking, and builds the confidence needed for the demands of Classes 9 and beyond.

Regular assessments and dedicated doubt-clearing sessions ensure each student progresses steadily and confidently.
