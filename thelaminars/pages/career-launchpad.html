Program Overview

Laminars Career Launchpad bridges the gap between education and employment. Whether you're a final-year student, a fresh graduate, or someone transitioning into tech — this program equips you with the practical skills and confidence needed to land a job in the software and technology field.

With a curriculum built around real industry expectations, students work on practical projects, receive interview training, and graduate with both knowledge and job-readiness.
