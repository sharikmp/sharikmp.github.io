English – Academic

Literature, writing skills, and grammar coaching for Classes 9–12 with a sharp focus on board exam performance and linguistic confidence.

Program Overview

English Academic is a board-focused coaching program for students in Classes 9–12. The course spans the full prescribed English syllabus — literature, writing, and grammar — and is structured to maximise marks while developing genuine language skills.

Students receive chapter-by-chapter literary analysis, extensive writing practice across all formats, and targeted grammar drills. Regular mock papers and question-type walkthroughs ensure students are fully prepared for the actual exam.

Topics Covered

Literature

Prose, poetry & readers

Writing Skills

Letters, articles, speeches, essays

Grammar

Tenses, clauses, error correction

Mock Papers

Board-pattern full practice tests

What Makes It Special

Chapter-Wise Literature

Every lesson from Flamingo, Vistas, First Flight and Footprints explained in depth with key questions and answers.

Writing Format Mastery

Formal and informal letters, data interpretation, notice writing, and creative formats — all covered with templates and practice.

Grammar Accuracy

Focused sessions on grammar rules, editing, gap-filling, and sentence transformation — the topics that cost students marks.

Exam-Focused Practice

Regular mock tests and previous-year question walkthroughs prepare students thoroughly for the actual board paper.

Who Is This For?

  • Students in Classes 9, 10, 11, and 12 preparing for CBSE or state board exams
  • Students who find grammar or writing formats confusing
  • Learners aiming to improve their English scores significantly
  • Anyone who wants to build a strong command of academic English
